Tourist Attraction

Bukit Cabang is a hilly area often known for its goats ("kambing"). The place is more rural and farm-like, and you might see goats grazing around, adding to the authentic countryside feel.

Wang Kelian is right by the Thai border and is famous for its weekend markets. It’s the place to be if you want to cross over for some shopping or grab some cheap goods. Plus, it’s surrounded by nice limestone hills and forests.

Bukit Ayer is one of those spots where locals go to relax, especially since it’s home to a recreational forest with cool waterfalls and picnic areas. It’s a great spot to chill out or even take a dip in the natural pools.
